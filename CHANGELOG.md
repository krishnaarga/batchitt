### 📘 How to Login (New)

**Q: How to get Private Key?**

1. Open https://batchitt.com by clicking on **"Open Login Page"**
2. Log in using **Google**
3. You will receive your **Private Key** on the dashboard
4. Copy the Private Key
5. Paste it into the input box inside the extension
6. Click on **Sign In**
