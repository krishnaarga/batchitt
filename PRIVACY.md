# Privacy Policy for Batchitt

**Last Updated:** April 12, 2026

---

## 📌 Introduction

Batchitt is a developer collaboration platform that enables real-time communication through a Visual Studio Code extension and web application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how your data is handled.

---

## 📊 Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information
When you sign in using Google OAuth, we collect:
- Name
- Email address
- Profile picture

This information is used solely to identify you within the Batchitt platform.

---

### 2. Collaboration Data
To provide chat functionality, we process:
- One-to-one and group chat messages
- Shared content such as text and code snippets
- Message timestamps

---

### 3. Technical Data
We may collect limited technical information such as:
- VS Code version
- Basic environment details

This helps us ensure compatibility and improve performance.

---

## 🔐 How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for the following purposes:

- Authenticate and manage your user session  
- Enable real-time communication between users  
- Sync messages and contacts across devices  
- Improve product performance and user experience  

---

## 🔒 Data Security

Security is a core part of Batchitt.

- All communication is transmitted securely using modern protocols (e.g., WebSockets via Socket.IO)  
- Messages and data are protected using production-grade security practices  
- Unique identifiers (such as keys or tokens) are used to maintain secure sessions  

We continuously work to improve our security practices.

---

## 🚫 What We Do NOT Do

- ❌ We do NOT sell your personal data  
- ❌ We do NOT share your data with third parties for marketing  
- ❌ We do NOT access or read your local files or source code  
- ❌ We do NOT track your coding activity inside VS Code  

---

## 🌐 Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third-party services to provide core functionality:

- **Google OAuth** for authentication  
  - We never access your Google password  
  - We only receive basic profile information  

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

---

## 🗂️ Data Retention

- Chat data may be stored to provide message history and continuity  
- Logs and technical data are retained only as long as necessary for debugging and performance improvements  

---

## 🧑‍💻 User Responsibility

You are responsible for:
- Keeping your account secure  
- Avoiding sharing sensitive or confidential information in chats  

---
